All about our study buddy system




Study with buddies


We're here to help provid a virtual study partner experience. Sometimes we all need a little help keeping our focus, and a study buddy is the perfect way to help keep each other on track!





Frequently Asked Questions


Who can see my information?

Your email will remain private to you, but any one else can see information such as your major and your school


I'm a high school student, can I sign up?

Yes of course! You can indicate 'HS 2022' as your graduation for example, and put your main concentration of study or main interest as your major. We hope you can gain advice from college students and each other as well!


Do I have to pay for this service?

Nope, our website is completely free! We are purely here to help you find buddies
