Study with buddies


We're here to help provide a virtual study partner experience. In today's world it can be hard to find a friend to study with, especially given the pandemic. Sometimes we all need a little help keeping our focus, or an outside perspective to help solve our homework assignments. A study buddy is the perfect way to help keep each other on track!


Study with people from within the same specialization or school. Enhance your learning eperience by helping your buddy and yourself solve problems or tackle issues collaboratively.
